About

A herald with a song in her heart.

Faith Abdulsalam is a songwriter, music artiste, and health coach with a passion for inspiring people to live purposefully and care for their bodies through conscious healthy choices. Through Keen Health Australia, she encourages and supports others on their journey toward better health and fulfilled purpose.

Born in 1984 in Osun State, Nigeria, Faith began writing songs at the age of twelve and now writes and ministers from Queensland, Australia. She holds a B.Tech degree in Food Science and Technology from LAUTECH. Faith is married to Toyin Abdulsalam, and together they are blessed with three children.
